Training & Documentation

The IT department helps staff and students learn how to use digital systems by:

  • Training sessions: Workshops or classes that teach how to use software, tools, or devices.
  • Creating guides: Writing simple instructions or manuals to help people understand systems.
  • FAQs and help pages: Online pages that answer common questions.
  • Video tutorials: Short videos showing how to perform tasks step by step.
  • Support services: Help desks and chat support to answer questions anytime.

This helps everyone use technology easily and confidently.
