Ensuring Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity means protecting computers, networks, and data from bad people who want to steal or damage information. The IT department does this by:

  • Firewalls: These act like guards to stop unauthorized access to the network.
  • Antivirus software: Programs that detect and remove viruses or malware.
  • Network monitoring: Watching the network to find strange or dangerous activity quickly.
  • Regular updates: Keeping software and systems up to date to fix security holes.
  • User education: Teaching staff and students about safe internet habits like strong passwords and avoiding suspicious emails.

By doing all this, the IT department helps keep the institution’s data safe and systems running smoothly.
