Maintaining Hardware

Maintaining hardware means taking care of all the physical devices used by the institution so they work properly and last longer. This includes:

  • Servers: Powerful computers that store data and run important services.
  • Printers: Devices for printing documents, which need regular checking and fixing.
  • Lab computers: Computers used by students and staff in computer labs.
  • Networking devices: Routers, switches, and cables that connect computers to the internet and to each other.
  • Other equipment: Projectors, scanners, keyboards, mice, and monitors.

The IT team regularly cleans hardware, replaces broken parts, updates drivers, and troubleshoots problems to keep everything running smoothly.
